SINA is an artificial intelligence application, which can help you define about your charisma, character, personality, your interested area of goals and your weaker side as well. SINA – Means ‘YOU’ in finnish language. This patented product is owned and operated by Trackon, Finland.

SINA – is considered as one of the most thought provoking aptitude tests that available in the market now. This aptitude test designed to determine a person’s ability in their skill or field of knowledge which will guide the student to pick up their desired studies which can fit well for their future shaping. The result discloses the innate taste of each test taker in detail.


This software measures multiple intelligences by asking 24 closed questions. Your answers to these questions are the conscious response of your emotions, body language, gestures , eye contacts, your overall aura analysis. This software also track your psycho physiological changes that stimuli of the questions elicit. These are known as the unconscious response based on a cumulative average of both, the software shows the level of each intelligence.

Results will include: Intelligence profile(Single broad ability like reading, knowledge of vocabulary), broad field (social and natural science), Narrow Field (Narrow Personality traits like distinguishing quality or characteristic) and skills by that students will understand what they are interested in or not.

Salient features of SINA

  • Sina, a thought-provoking aptitude test, utilises Artificial Intelligence alongside a camera to determine students’ interests and skill sets.
  • Designed to determine a student’s preferred field of knowledge or ability in a skill, Sina’s patented technology enables students to understand their strong suits and pick up their desired course of study in the future.
  • As deciding on a career can be quite challenging, Sina aims to make the process easier so that students can embark on a career they will genuinely love.
  • The test includes 24 closed questions, taking less than 15 minutes. The students are seated in front of their laptops or computers with the camera on, ensuring they are focused on their answers for accurate results.
  • Sina analyses both conscious and subconscious responses. The answers given proactively are the conscious response, whereas the psycho-physiological changes that the stimuli of the questions elicit are the subconscious response.
  • Sina determines the innate taste of each student by taking a cumulative average of both the conscious and subconscious responses.
  • The results come in the form of a schedule specifying four categories:
    1. Intelligence profile (single broad ability like reading, knowledge of vocabulary)
    2. Broad field (social and natural sciences)
    3. Narrow field (narrow personality traits like distinguishing qualities or characteristics)
    4. Skills

    The results are handed over to the student in a closed envelope to discuss with their family later. Sina offers valuable insights into one’s interests, further providing good-suited career suggestions.
